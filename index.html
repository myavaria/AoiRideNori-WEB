By using HiCa, you agree to this policy and consent to our data practices as described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our ride-sharing and rental services.

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register or use HiCa, we collect your name, email, phone number, password, driver’s license (if applicable), and payment details.
  • Profile and Preferences: Optional information such as profile picture, preferred language, vehicle preferences, and communication settings.
  • Ride and Rental Details: Pickup and drop-off locations, rental duration, trip history, and communication with drivers or customers.
  • Customer Support: Information you provide when contacting support, such as inquiries, feedback, or complaint details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Details about how you interact with the app—features used, session duration, and performance data.
  • Device and Technical Data: Device model, operating system, browser type, IP address, and mobile network information.
  • Location Data: With your consent, we collect GPS data to provide core functionality such as trip routing, driver matching, and vehicle tracking. You can control location permissions through your device settings.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive data from third-party partners, such as identity verification providers, payment processors, and marketing partners, to ensure compliance, process transactions, and enhance user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining the Services, including ride booking, vehicle rentals, and account management.
  • Processing payments and preventing fraudulent transactions.
  • Facilitating communication between riders, drivers, and support staff.
  • Improving app performance, developing new features, and analyzing usage trends.
  • Sending updates, service notifications, and promotional messages (only if you opt in).
  • Ensuring safety and security, verifying user identity, and complying with applicable laws.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location, we process data under the following legal bases:

  • To fulfill a contract (e.g., providing rides or rentals).
  • With your consent (for example, for marketing or location sharing).
  • For our legitimate interests (to improve service and security).
  • To comply with legal obligations (such as tax and safety regulations).

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share information only as necessary and in accordance with this policy.
